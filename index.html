2. Information We Collect

Our Extension is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information. Specifically:

  • No Personal Information: We do not require registration, login, or any personal details to use the Extension.
  • No Tracking or Analytics: We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.
  • Local Processing: All video-to-audio conversions occur locally within your browser. Your files never leave your device.

2. How the Extension Works

The Extension converts video files to MP3 audio format directly within your browser using the Web Audio API and MediaRecorder API. The conversion process is as follows:

  • You select or drag-and-drop a video file into the Extension interface.
  • The Extension processes the video locally in your browser.
  • The audio stream is extracted and converted to MP3 format locally.
  • The resulting MP3 file is provided to you for download directly from your browser.

At no point during this process are your files uploaded to any external servers or third-party services.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: The Extension temporarily stores files and conversion data in your browser's local memory during the conversion process. Once the conversion is complete and the file is downloaded, temporary data is automatically cleared.
  • Security Measures: Since no data leaves your device, your files remain secure and private. We implement industry-standard security practices to ensure the Extension operates safely within your browser environment.
